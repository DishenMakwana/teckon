# Suryansh Website Comprehensive Analysis (thesuryansh.com)

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the website of one of your suppliers, **Suryansh**, operated under **J B S Industries India Pvt. Ltd.** in Gondal, Rajkot, Gujarat. 

Suryansh is a specialized manufacturer of hydraulic gear pumps and components, mainly serving construction, agriculture, water drilling (borewells), and industrial sectors.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Platform & Architecture
* **CMS & Framework:** WordPress (utilizing Elementor Page Builder).
* **E-Commerce/Catalog:** WooCommerce (configured as an inquiry/quote catalog instead of direct checkout).

### 3. Quality & Manufacturing (`/quality/`)
* **Tagline:** *Precision. Performance. Perfection.*
* **Detailed Technical Workflow:** Unlike standard quality lists, Suryansh showcases their exact manufacturing stages:
  1. **Design & Development:** Using **SolidWorks** engineering software for design, validation, stress analysis, and simulations.
  2. **Casting & Forging:** Shaping parts under extreme pressure to improve density, durability, and fatigue resistance.
  3. **Lab Testing (Material & Hardness):** Metallurgical analysis and spectrometry on raw materials before production; hardness tests to confirm structural toughness.
  4. **CNC & VMC Machining:** Advanced machining to achieve micrometric tolerances and surface finishes.
  5. **QC Inspection:** Full inspection using Coordinate Measuring Machines (**CMM**).
  6. **Gear Processing:** Hobbing, shaving, gear grinding, OD lapping, and face lapping for exact profiles.
  7. **Assembly:** Cleaning raw materials, QC check of parts, and assembly in clean, controlled environments.
  8. **Hydraulic Testing Bench:** Every pump is bench-tested on certified testing rigs to verify volumetric efficiency and pressure ratings.

### 2. Product Detail Page Specifications Table Structure
Every specific product page includes a robust technical table:
* **Part No:** Suryansh internal code (e.g., `SGP-NM01012919A16`).
* **OEM Part No:** Direct manufacturer code (e.g., `20/925588`).
* **CC:** Displacement (e.g., `29/19/16` or `NA`).
* **Product Type:** Category identifier (e.g., `Hydraulic Gear Pump`).
* **Application:** Machinery model (e.g., `Telehandler`, `Backhoe Loader`).
* **Body Material:** Structural alloy (e.g., `Gray Cast Iron`).
* **Direction of Rotation:** e.g., `Anti-Clockwise` or `Clockwise`.
* **Usage:** Action context (e.g., *"Boom lifting, extending, tilting and steering"*).
* **Brand:** *Suryansh*.
* **Country of Origin:** *Make in India*.
